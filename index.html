Best of Canton Neighbors

The best posts on the Canton Neighbors Facebook group, a neighborhood Facebook group in Baltimore, Maryland.

The group was founded on March 21, 2012 by Canton Community Association as a message board for the community. It has since grown to over 20,000 members and is the de facto community watercooler. The posts listed below are some of the community favorites over the years.

This list is compiled by admin Elliott Plack. Message him with any suggestions of posts to add.
